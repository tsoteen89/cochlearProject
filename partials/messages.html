This tabs usage

I wanted to provide an easy way for patients to easily message their providers "Patient to Provider messaging". With this tab, a patient can view their providers picture, and possibly have a detailed description of exactly what their providers expertise is.

Visually, it would look similar to Sanan's Care Team tab which is why this tab may not be needed. Furthermore, this tab would not be beneficial for provider to provider messaging. If anybody can further explain to me why I should or why I shouldn't keep this tab available I'm all ears...
